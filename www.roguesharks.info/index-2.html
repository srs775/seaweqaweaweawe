OVERVIEW

WELCOME TO THE ROGUE SHARKS COALITION

RSC is a 3D high-quality collection of 5,000 Rogue Shark NFTs—unique digital collectibles swimming in the Solana blockchain. Your Rogue Shark doubles as your membership badge and grants access to sharks-only benefits, the first of which is access to our TREASURE CHEST lottery. Future areas and perks, like action figures and the Rogue Sharks Surf Club, can be unlocked by the community through roadmap activation.

DIGITAL ART DRIVING REAL SOCIAL CHANGE FOR OUR OCEAN

The ocean is what connects us all, so it's important to keep it healthy for this generation and the next. We are excited to support conservation organizations and collaborate with experts to drive meaningful change for our ocean and its species.

FAQ

LOOK HERE FOR ANSWERS

What is the total supply?

A total of 5,000 Great White Rogue Sharks will be created. We will release collections in the future for other species of Rogue Sharks such as HammerHead Sharks and Mutant Sharks. You can learn more about this in our Roadmap!

‍

‍Psstt... Don't worry if you aren't able to mint a shark. Everyone who participates in our launch will receive a participatory collectible that has utility in the Rogue Sharks ecosystem.

How much will it cost to mint an NFT?

We will be using a Fair Launch Lottery, which will allow our community to bid on a range between 3 SOL and 4 SOL. The median of all bids will be the fair price of a Rogue Shark. You can learn more about how Fair Launch Lotteries work by watching this video we made here.


Will there be a pre-sale?

No, but we will make sure active community members are rewarded. More information will be released closer to launch.

Is there a limit to how many Rogue Sharks I can mint?

1 Shark per wallet.

Do you have a rarity system?

Of course! Rarity is extremely important to the Rogue Sharks. Our traits are ranked from “Common” to “Legendary”. You learn more by accessing our Traits & Rarity document. We will be adding a section to our website soon where you can see all the traits and rarity.

Will there be a secondary market?

Yes, we will be listing on Solanart or Digital Eyes as soon as we go live so you can trade with fellow Rogue Sharks immediately.

Will there be a community fund?

Yes! The Rogue Sharks will be placing 15% of our sales and secondary market royalties towards community initiatives. This fund will ensure we are able to grow the Rogue Sharks ecosystem and community, and continue to innovate in the NFT space. A meaningful portion of the 15% will be allocated towards ocean conservation. We are working with experts to put together a sound plan for charitable donations.

Are there secondary sale royalties?

Yes, royalties are set at 4.1%. As mentioned above, we will be contributing 15% of this back into the Rogue Sharks community fund.

Do I own my Shark after purchasing it?

Full intellectual property rights are granted to the buyer.

Wen Mint?

We will launch on October 9, 2021 at 9 PM UTC
